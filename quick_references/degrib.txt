DEGRIB commands

-----------------
degrib psurge.t2017011212z.al832017_e10_inc_dat.conus_625m.grib2  -C -msg 1 -Flt -Interp -GrADS

---------
degrib psurge.t2017011212z.al832017_e10_inc_dat.conus_625m.grib2 -I

Convert to shape
----------------
degrib psurge.t2017011212z.al832017_e10_inc_dat.conus_625m.grib2 -C -msg 1 -Shp
